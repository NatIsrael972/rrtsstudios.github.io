    Brief History

    RRTS Studios is established by mark4211 and SMB237U when development started for the Northern Region Line, an addon for openBVE/TSF, in end-2012.
    In March 2014, RRTS Studios embarked on another project, Rexington City, and addon for OMSI 2. However, development ended in July 2014 due to a corruption of the files.
    In August 2014, development started again for an addon for OMSI 2, Woodlands City. TIB 1031L joined RRTS Studios, creating repaints for buses. It still remains in beta testing phase.
